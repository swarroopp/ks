EXPLORE THE COSMOS

Join VNR VJIET's premier space club - Kaksya Sastra. We explore the mysteries of the universe through research, innovation, and collaboration.

ABOUT OUR CLUB

Kaksya Sastra is the official space club of VNR VJIET, dedicated to fostering interest in space science, astronomy, and aerospace technology among students.

Our Mission

To inspire the next generation of space explorers through hands-on projects, research, and collaboration with industry experts.

Our Vision

To establish VNR VJIET as a hub for space technology innovation and contribute to India's growing space sector.

Our Values

Curiosity, Innovation, Collaboration, and Excellence in everything we do to explore the final frontier.
